One of the most popular and top-performing hot drink machines.
The Kikko was Necta’s first hot drink machine and remains one of the best-selling across Europe thanks to its simplicity, reliability and versatility. With an elegant design, Necta’s Kikko is suitable for any professional location desiring an uncomplicated and dependable hot drink vending solution. It’s also been built to work in co-ordination with Necta’s Snakky and Snakky SL for those who wish to offer a complete vending service, especially where space might be limited.

Kikko Max is the taller version of the well-known Kikko from Necta. Designed to be banked next to the Snakky Max spiral machine and other Necta machines, Kikko Max forms one half of the perfect vending partnership.

As well as a change of height, Kikko Max’s selection and payment area has been updated, creating a more user-friendly experience. An increased cup capacity guarantees the ability to cater for individual tastes. The Kikko solution is designed for medium sized locations.
