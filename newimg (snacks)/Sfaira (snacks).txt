Necta Sfera

Offering protection and service in inside vending.
The Sfera can offer a wide variety of food products, snacks, bottles and cans. With an excellent capacity to size ratio, the machine is able to vend a broader selection of products and allow for a greater variety of individual tastes. With its elegant and refined design and wide backlit display, the user has maximum visibility of the product, making Sfera excellent distribution solution for crowded places where quick service is essential.

Necta Sfera Key Features
Specially designed for inside use
Proven protection against vandalism
Flexible layout to offer both snack and food products
Specially designed tray for sandwiches and baguettes
Armored user interface with metallic numeric keypad
Shatterproof window frontage
Metal coin insert and change return area
Dispensing compartment with blocking device
